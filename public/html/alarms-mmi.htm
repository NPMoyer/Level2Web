HSM Level 2 MMI Alarms

Critical (Red) Alarms

  • WARNING - stand WR diameters differ by number mils - verify rolls

    • Verify that the data in Roll Inventory is correct for this stand Top and bottom work roll diameter difference should be less than 5 mils
  • ERROR - Invalid stand BR diameter

    • Backup roll diameters must be between 42 and 46 inches for each stand
  • ERROR - Invalid stand WR diameter

    • Work roll diameters be between 19.25 and 22.25 inches for each stand

Informational (White) Alarms

  • Roll extraction completed by name

    • A roll has been extracted successfully and level 2 data is updated
  • Roll change completed by name

    • A roll change has been entered successfully and level 2 data is updated
